About Simoneaux Project Management

Multifaceted construction programs present complex challenges that require successful team integration, making building the right team to manage your project crucial to your success. We focus on the big picture, establishing priorities and coordinating activities and participants to ensure that your projects are delivered safely, on time, and within budget. We identify issues and resolve conflicts using established procedures and processes that consistently yield excellent results. Our expertise in representing owners through all phases of the planning, design, and construction process has culminated in the successful completion of more than $100 billion in projects.
