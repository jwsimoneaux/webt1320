Welcome to Simoneaux Project Management

Simoneaux Project Management is a construction consulting firm, an integrated team of skilled professionals who deliver solutions to your project challenges by providing innovative, construction-focused program and project management services. As project management consultants, we tailor teams, resources, and systems to your projects with consistent positive results.

We pride ourselves on building teams of accomplished professionals with extensive real-world construction management experience. SPM people are driven to continuously learn, lead, and problem-solve to achieve personal and professional growth. We are intent on fully understanding project issues before leveraging multiple perspectives and knowledge sources to arrive at a solution that best fits a project or program.

Whether you are an owner, contractor, attorney, surety, or other construction professional, we can assist you in achieving project success from the start to finish.
