Joshua & Samantha Simoneaux

Our Story

Joshua and Samantha met in Midland, TX while both were working in the oil field. When they both approached one another on a birthday party for the both of them, jaws dropped to the floor when they realized each others ages! But from that moment on, the age difference that was not even a thing. Joshua proposed to Samantha late afternoon on March 4, 2020 from the comfort of their home after Joshua's neck surgery. Joshua didn't want to wait another day without asking and Samantha had a hunch and was bound to push question out. Shortly after announcing their engagement to friends and family, Covid-19 had hit the United States and Joshua and Samantha were laid off from the oil field only for a new adventure to come.

They both nestled down in Chattanooga, TN where their lives had completely done a one-eighty. They both were bound and driven for a fresh start and began attending school through a local community college and had began making so many wonderful friends along the way. After over two years of living in Tennessee, their final destination was to move back to Texas where they both love to be. Now living north of Dallas, the adventure continues on with the planning of a wedding, a graduation and a drive into their careers with not only each other but their two four legged children Charlie and Daisy as well. There is so much set into the future for Samantha and Joshua but only together can they accomplish this, and together they will do just that and more.
